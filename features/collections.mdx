Collections allow you to organize and save Bible verses that are meaningful to you. Create themed collections, build study lists, or compile verses for specific purposes.

<Note>
  Collections are a user-specific feature. You must be signed in to create and manage collections.
</Note>

## Creating Collections

Start organizing your verses by creating a collection:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign In">
    Make sure you're signed in to your account.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate to Collections">
    Go to the Collections page from the main navigation.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create New Collection">
    Click the "Create Collection" or similar button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Name Your Collection">
    Enter a descriptive name for your collection (e.g., "Comfort and Peace", "Verses on Faith", "Wedding Ceremony").
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save">
    Submit the form to create your collection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Card title="Collection Ideas" icon="lightbulb">
  - **Thematic collections**: Verses about love, hope, faith, etc.
  - **Study collections**: Passages for a specific Bible study
  - **Memorization lists**: Verses you're working to memorize
  - **Event preparation**: Scripture for weddings, funerals, or holidays
  - **Personal encouragement**: Verses that speak to your current season
</Card>

## Adding Verses to Collections

You can add verses to your collections from multiple places in the application:

### From Bible Reading

While reading any chapter:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Your Verse">
    Navigate to the chapter containing the verse you want to save.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Add Button">
    Click the add/bookmark icon next to the verse.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Collection">
    A dialog appears showing your collections. Choose which collection to add the verse to.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm">
    The verse is added immediately with its full text, reference, and Bible version.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### From Search Results

When searching for verses:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Execute Search">
    Search for keywords or phrases to find relevant verses.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add from Results">
    Click the add button next to any verse in the search results.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Collection">
    Select the collection where you want to store the verse.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  When adding a verse, the complete verse text, reference (book, chapter, verse), and Bible translation ID are all saved. This ensures you can always view the verse in its original translation.
</Info>

## Viewing Collections

Access and read your saved verses:

### Collection List

The Collections page displays:

- **Collection name** - The title you gave the collection
- **Verse count** - How many verses are saved in each collection
- **Creation date** - When you created the collection
- **Quick actions** - Edit or delete options

### Collection Details

Click on any collection to view its contents:

- **Full verse text** - The complete Scripture text you saved
- **Verse reference** - Book, chapter, and verse number
- **Link to context** - Click the reference to read the verse in its full chapter
- **Bible version** - Which translation the verse is from

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Card View">
    Verses are displayed in card format with:
    - Clear, readable text
    - Prominent verse reference
    - Remove button for quick deletion
    - Hover effects for better interactivity
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="List View">
    Some layouts may offer a list view for:
    - Compact display of multiple verses
    - Quick scanning of content
    - Easy printing or copying
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Managing Collections

### Editing Collections

Update your collection details:

1. **Navigate to Collections** page
2. **Find the collection** you want to edit
3. **Click edit/settings** icon
4. **Update the name** or other details
5. **Save changes**

<Note>
  Editing a collection updates its name and metadata but does not affect the verses stored within it.
</Note>

### Deleting Collections

Remove collections you no longer need:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate Collection">
    Find the collection you want to delete.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Delete">
    Click the delete button (typically a trash icon).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Confirm that you want to permanently delete the collection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Deleting a collection permanently removes it and all verses within it. This action cannot be undone.
</Warning>

## Managing Individual Verses

### Removing Verses

Remove individual verses from a collection:

1. **Open the collection** containing the verse
2. **Find the verse** you want to remove
3. **Click the remove button** (typically an X or trash icon)
4. The verse is immediately removed from the collection

<Info>
  Removing a verse from a collection doesn't delete the verse from the Bible or from other collections where it might be saved.
</Info>

### Viewing Verse Context

Jump from a saved verse to its full chapter:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Collection">
    View the collection containing your saved verse.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Verse Reference">
    Click on the verse reference (e.g., "John 3:16").
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Read in Context">
    You're taken to the Bible reading page with the verse highlighted in its full chapter.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Organizing Your Verses

### Best Practices

<Card title="Tips for Effective Collections" icon="star">
  - **Use descriptive names**: "Encouragement" is better than "Collection 1"
  - **Keep collections focused**: Themed collections are easier to use than catch-all lists
  - **Regular maintenance**: Review and update collections periodically
  - **Multiple collections**: Don't hesitate to save the same verse in multiple relevant collections
  - **Note-taking**: Consider using the Notes feature alongside collections for deeper study
</Card>

### Collection Strategies

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Thematic">

    - Love and Compassion

  <Tab title="Personal">

  <Tab title="Study-Based">

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Privacy and Storage

Your collections are:

- **Private** - Only visible to you when signed in
- **Secure** - Stored securely in the Convex database
- **Persistent** - Available across all your devices
- **Backed up** - Protected by automatic database backups

<Info>
  Collections are tied to your user account. Sign in from any device to access your saved verses.
</Info>
